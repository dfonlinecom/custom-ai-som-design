**Combat:**
- Kill enemy agent: +100 XP

**Missions:**
- Complete training mission: +200 XP
- Complete field mission: +500 XP

- Complete impossible mission: +5,000 XP

**Social:**
- Recruit new agent: +500 XP

**Daily Challenges:**
- Complete daily challenge: +200 XP
