## ðŸ’¡ Key Innovations

### **1. Flip-Down Projection Panel (Pro & Ultimate Tiers)**

A spring-loaded, magnetic panel that flips down from the front of the device to serve as a dedicated projection surface for the laser projector.

**Mechanism:**
-   **Closed:** The panel sits flush, protecting the 3.4" AMOLED screen.
-   **Activation:** A small button releases a magnetic latch.
-   **Deployment:** A custom spring hinge smoothly swings the panel down to 110 degrees.
-   **Projection:** The laser projector automatically turns on and projects a 4-inch diagonal image onto the panel.
-   **Stowing:** The user pushes the panel up until it clicks back into place.

**Specifications:**
-   **Projection Surface:** 4.0-inch diagonal (3.5" x 2.0")
-   **Material:** Rigid polymer with "Digital Grey Lite" high-gain coating
-   **Backing:** Microfiber cloth to clean and protect the AMOLED screen when closed
-   **Cost:** +$30 per unit

**Why This is Brilliant:**
-   **Always-available projection surface** - No need to hold your hand up or find a wall.
-   **Protects the main screen** - The panel acts as a cover when closed.
-   **"Wow" factor** - A unique, mechanical feature that sets your device apart.
-   **Justifies premium pricing** - A clear, tangible upgrade for the Pro and Ultimate tiers.
