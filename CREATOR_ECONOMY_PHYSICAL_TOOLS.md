## 1. THE VISION: CREATORS ARE THE PLATFORM

PlayPod is not a game. It is a **platform for creators to build the future of entertainment.**

We provide the hardware (PlayPod, glasses, watch) and the tools (Mission Creation Studio, 3D printing platform, AI SDK). Creators provide the content (missions, sensors, maps, gear, experiences). Together, we build a new medium of human connection and play.

**The Core Insight:**

> "The true evolution is allowing creators to create missions and earn from these missions and tactical things or their own 3D-printed devices and sensors. This way they can earn huge money by creating tactical maps. This is a complete transformation of entertainment."

This is **Roblox** (user-generated content) + **Etsy** (physical goods marketplace) + **YouTube** (creator revenue share) + **Fortnite** (in-game purchases) combined into a single, real-world platform.
