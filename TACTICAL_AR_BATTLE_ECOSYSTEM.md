## 2. The Proximity Sensor AR Combat System

### **How Real-World AR Battles Work:**

**The Technology:**
- **Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE)** for proximity detection
- **IR transmitters** on PlayPod device (like laser tag guns)
- **IR receivers** on battle vest (hit detection)
- **GPS + AR** for location-based battles

**The Gameplay Loop:**

1. **Pre-Battle Setup:**
   - Kids equip battle vests
   - Load magazines into pouches
   - Set team colors in PlayPod app
   - Choose game mode (Team Deathmatch, Capture the Flag, King of the Hill)

2. **Battle Start:**
   - PlayPod devices detect nearby players via BLE
   - AR overlay shows enemy positions on screen
   - Health bar displays on vest LEDs

3. **Combat:**
   - Kid selects attack card from magazine
   - Slaps card onto PlayPod device
   - **PlayPod emits IR beam** (like laser tag)
   - Aims device at enemy player
   - **Enemy vest sensors detect hit**
   - Enemy's health decreases (shown on their vest LEDs)
   - Vibration feedback confirms hit

4. **Taking Damage:**
   - When hit, vest vibrates
   - Health bar LEDs decrease
   - PlayPod screen shows damage taken
   - Use health card to heal

5. **Victory:**
   - Last player/team standing wins
   - XP and rewards distributed
   - Stats tracked in PlayPod app

---

### **Game Modes:**

**1. Team Deathmatch**
- 2-4 teams

**2. Capture the Flag**

## 5. The Sensor Technology

### **IR Transmitter (on PlayPod):**
- **IR LED** (940nm wavelength)
- **Range:** 50 feet
- **Beam width:** 15 degrees
- **Modulated signal** (unique player ID encoded)
- **Cost:** $2 per unit

### **IR Receivers (on Battle Vest):**
- **8x IR photodiodes** (strategic locations)
- **360-degree coverage**
- **Decode player ID** from IR signal
- **Send hit data to PlayPod via Bluetooth**
- **Cost:** $5 per vest

### **Bluetooth Communication:**
- **BLE 5.0** (low power)
- **Range:** 100 feet
- **Mesh network** (up to 32 players)
- **Real-time sync** (hit detection, health, position)

### **GPS + AR:**
- **GPS** for outdoor location tracking
- **AR overlay** shows enemy positions
- **Geo-fencing** for game boundaries
- **Works with Google Maps AR**
