## 3. THE "CHOOSE YOUR IMPACT" INTERFACE

Upon setting up a Tier 3-6 device, users are presented with the "Choose Your Impact" screen. This is the heart of the donation model.

[Image: A clean, elegant UI mockup for the PlayPod screen or Tactical Glasses HUD. It shows a list of nonprofit logos and a slider.]

### **Step 1: Choose Your Cause**

Users select a nonprofit partner to receive their donated compute power. The list is categorized for easy browsing:

*   **Global Health:** World Health Organization, Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, St. Jude Children's Research Hospital (for protein folding).
*   **Environmental:** The Ocean Cleanup, NASA Climate Research, The Nature Conservancy.
*   **Humanitarian Aid:** Red Cross, Doctors Without Borders, World Food Programme.
*   **Scientific Discovery:** SETI@home, Folding@home, Einstein@Home.

### **Step 2: Choose Your Contribution Level**

A simple slider allows users to set their donation preference:

*   **30% Donation (Recommended):**
    *   *User Retains:* ~47 TOPS (still more powerful than a standard Tier 4 device).
    *   *Nonprofit Receives:* 20 TOPS (a dedicated AI accelerator, running 24/7).
    *   *UI Text:* "Contribute a significant portion of your device's power while retaining more than enough for all gaming and professional features."

*   **50% Donation (Maximum Impact):**
    *   *User Retains:* ~34 TOPS (still powerful enough for most applications).
    *   *Nonprofit Receives:* 33.5 TOPS.
    *   *UI Text:* "Become a key contributor to your chosen cause. Your device will be a major node in the global research network."

*   **100% Idle Donation (Default):**
    *   When the device is not in active use (e.g., overnight), 100% of its compute power (67 TOPS) is automatically donated.
    *   This has zero impact on the user's active experience.
    *   *UI Text:* "Maximize your impact without affecting performance. Your device will work for good while you sleep."

## 5. NONPROFIT PARTNERSHIPS & ENTERPRISE MODEL

This model creates a powerful B2B and B2G (Business-to-Government) revenue stream.

### **The Partnership Tiers:**

*   **Tier 1: Platform Partner (Free)**
    *   Any verified 501(c)(3) nonprofit can join the "Choose Your Impact" list.
    *   They can submit computational tasks to the general donation pool.

*   **Tier 2: Priority Partner ($10,000/month)**
    *   Guaranteed access to a specific amount of compute power (e.g., 1 PetaFLOP-hour per day).
    *   Priority task scheduling.
    *   Dedicated technical support from PlayPod engineers.

*   **Tier 3: Hardware Deployment Partner ($50,000 - $500,000+ per project)**
    *   PlayPod provides and deploys a dedicated fleet of Tier 5-6 devices for a specific project (e.g., deploying 100 Supercompute nodes for a disaster response agency).
    *   Includes custom sensor integration, on-site training, and maintenance.

This transforms our business model, adding a highly profitable and stable enterprise revenue stream alongside consumer sales.

## 6. UPDATED SWARM ARCHITECTURE

The Swarm Compute network now intelligently allocates resources based on donation choices.

**The Process:**
1.  A user selects the "Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation" and a "50% Donation."
2.  Their PlayPod is now tagged as a "Gates Foundation Node."
3.  50% of its compute cycles (33.5 TOPS) are permanently allocated to the Gates Foundation's private resource pool.
4.  The remaining 50% (33.5 TOPS) is available for the user's personal tasks.
5.  When the device is idle, the full 67 TOPS are directed to the Gates Foundation pool.
6.  The Gates Foundation can log into their portal and see their total available compute power in real-time, and submit tasks accordingly.

This creates dedicated, private supercomputers for each nonprofit partner, powered by a global network of citizen scientists.
